What is a Market?

In the traditional sense, the term 'market' refers to the place where buyers and sellers gather to enter into transactions involving the exchange of goods and services. It is in this sense that this term is being used in day to day language, even today. The other ways in which this term is being used in day to day language context of product market(cotton market, gold or share market), geographic market(national and international market), type of buyers (consumer market and industrial market) and the quantity of goods transacted(retail market and wholesale market)
