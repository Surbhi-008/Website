About Marketing Management

In order to achieve desired exchange outcomes Marketing Manas with target markets, it is important to decide what marketing management philosophy (or thinking or concept or orientation) should guide the marketing efforts of an organisation. An understanding of the marketing management philosophy to be adopted is important as it determines the emphasis or the weightage to be put on different factors, in achieving the organisational objectives. For example, whether the marketing efforts of an organisation will focus on the product, say designing its features etc. or on selling techniques or on customer's needs or the social concerns.
